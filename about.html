About

Panacea project

Panacea was born from the idea to become the standard tool for generating all the new Rails projects in michelada.io

Panacea aims to reduce the time you spent in the setup of a brand new Rails application.

It also attempts to improve your application’s development experience, by integrating the Rubocop linter, enforcing a minimum coverage and the possibility of running a Git hook to reduce the amount of broken builds or bad quality pull requests.

Most of the gems installed by Panacea are optional and at the end you have total control over your application’s Gemfile. For instance, if you don’t want to use interactor-rails, just remove it from your app’s Gemfile.

michelada.io

The Panacea project was developed during the Michelada Project Match, which is a competition between michelada’s employees to deliver software products that try to solve a known problem.
